What GeoPageScan audits

What is LLM Optimization?

We check for an llms.txt file, clear entity definitions and FAQ content that AI engines can quote directly when answering questions about you.

What is Structured Data and Schema?

We detect JSON-LD and the Schema.org types — FAQPage, Organization, LocalBusiness, Article, HowTo and AggregateRating — that make your content machine-readable to AI.

How does AI Crawlability work?

We verify that AI bots can actually reach you by checking robots.txt rules for GPTBot, ClaudeBot and PerplexityBot, your sitemap, and whether key content is hidden behind JavaScript or video.

What are Authority Signals?

We evaluate entity authority: named authors and founders, review and rating schema, About-page quality and external profile links such as LinkedIn and Wikidata.

What is Content Clarity?

We score direct-answer structure, question-led headings and the balance of informational versus promotional copy, because AI engines favour clear, factual content.

What is Technical SEO?

We check the classic signals AI still relies on: canonical tags, hreflang, Open Graph, mobile viewport and Core Web Vitals.

Frequently asked questions

What is GEO and how is it different from SEO?

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the practice of making your site easy for AI engines like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and Perplexity to understand and cite. Traditional SEO optimizes for blue-link rankings; GEO optimizes for being the answer the AI gives.

Is GeoPageScan really free?

Yes. Enter any URL with no login and no credit card, and you get a full scored report across six categories with prioritized, actionable fixes.

What is an llms.txt file?

llms.txt is a simple markdown file at your domain root that gives AI assistants a clean, structured summary of your entity, services and key pages. It is one of the highest-impact, lowest-effort GEO improvements you can make.

Which AI crawlers does the audit check for?

We check robots.txt access for the major AI user-agents including GPTBot, ChatGPT-User, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot and Google-Extended, plus whether you allow them to cite you.

How long does a scan take?

Most scans complete in a few seconds. Complex sites audited with the AI engine can take up to 60 to 90 seconds for the deepest analysis.

Will this work for non-English or local businesses?

Absolutely. The audit flags multilingual gaps such as missing hreflang and checks LocalBusiness signals that matter for local AI queries.
