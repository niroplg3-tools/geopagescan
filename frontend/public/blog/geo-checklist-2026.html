The Complete GEO Checklist for 2026

A single, printable checklist covering every GEO, AEO and SEO signal that affects whether AI cites your site.

By GeoPageScan·Jul 20, 2026·4 min read

Foundations

  • Publish and maintain llms.txt.
  • Allow AI crawlers; ship and submit a sitemap.
  • Serve key content as crawlable HTML, not JS-only.

Structure & entity

  • Organization + FAQPage schema, validated.
  • sameAs links and named authors (Person schema).
  • Marked-up reviews (AggregateRating).

Content & technical

  • Question-led, answer-first content with specific facts.
  • Canonical, hreflang, Open Graph, viewport, html lang.
  • Green Core Web Vitals.

Frequently asked questions

What's on a complete GEO checklist?⌄
Foundations (llms.txt, crawler access, crawlable HTML), structure and entity (schema, sameAs, reviews), and content and technical (answer-first content, canonical/hreflang, Core Web Vitals).
How do I work through it?⌄
Tackle foundations first, then entity and structure, then content and technical — re-scanning to confirm each lift.
Is there a tool to check it for me?⌄
Yes. A free GeoPageScan audit checks most of this automatically and prioritizes the fixes.
