GEO for SaaS: How to Get Your Product Recommended by AI

When buyers ask AI "what's the best tool for X", SaaS products with clear entities and structured proof win. Here's the GEO playbook for SaaS.

By GeoPageScan·Jun 8, 2026·4 min read

AI is the new software shortlist

Buyers increasingly ask AI to compare and recommend tools before they ever visit a vendor site. If AI can't clearly understand what your product does and who it's for, you're left off the shortlist.

Define the product entity

State plainly what category you're in, who it's for and what problem you solve — in your hero, your About page and your llms.txt. Add Organization and Product/SoftwareApplication schema.

Vague positioning ("the platform for modern teams") gives AI nothing to match a query against.

Provide structured proof

Surface comparisons, integrations, pricing and reviews as real HTML (and AggregateRating schema). These are exactly the facts AI needs to recommend you with confidence.

Frequently asked questions

How do I get my SaaS recommended by AI?⌄
Define a clear product entity (category, audience, problem) in copy, schema and llms.txt, and provide structured proof — comparisons, pricing and reviews — as crawlable HTML.
What schema should a SaaS site use?⌄
Organization plus SoftwareApplication or Product, FAQPage for common questions, and AggregateRating for reviews.
Why isn't my SaaS mentioned by AI?⌄
Usually vague positioning and thin structured data. Make your category and audience explicit and back claims with crawlable, marked-up facts.
