Internal Linking for AI Crawlers and Topic Authority

Smart internal links help AI understand your topic structure and find every page. Here's how to do it well.

By GeoPageScan·Jul 6, 2026·4 min read

Links map your expertise

Internal links show AI and search how your content connects, reinforcing topical authority and ensuring every page is discoverable.

Build topic clusters

Create a pillar page for each core topic and link it to supporting articles (and back). This cluster structure signals depth and helps engines understand your focus.

Use descriptive anchors

Anchor text should describe the destination in plain language. It helps users, crawlers and AI understand what each linked page is about.

Frequently asked questions

How does internal linking help AI visibility?⌄
It helps AI discover every page and understand how your content connects, reinforcing topical authority around your core subjects.
What is a topic cluster?⌄
A pillar page on a core topic linked to and from supporting articles, signaling depth and structure to engines.
What anchor text should I use?⌄
Descriptive, plain-language anchors that say what the linked page is about — avoid generic 'click here'.
