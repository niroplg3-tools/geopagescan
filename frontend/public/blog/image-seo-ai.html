Image SEO for AI: Alt Text, Captions and Context

AI can't see your images — it reads the text around them. Here's how to make visual content count for AI visibility.

By GeoPageScan·Jul 16, 2026·4 min read

AI reads text, not pixels

Most crawlers and AI engines understand images through their alt text, captions, file names and surrounding context — not the pixels themselves.

Describe images well

Write descriptive alt text for meaningful images, add captions where useful, use clear file names, and place images near relevant text.

Mark up key images

Use ImageObject in your schema for important visuals (logos, product shots) so engines can associate them with your entity.

Frequently asked questions

Does image SEO help AI visibility?⌄
Yes, indirectly. AI reads alt text, captions and surrounding context, so well-described images add to the signals it understands.
What makes good alt text?⌄
A concise, accurate description of the image's content and purpose — not keyword stuffing.
Should I use ImageObject schema?⌄
For important images like logos and product shots, yes — it helps engines associate them with your entity.
