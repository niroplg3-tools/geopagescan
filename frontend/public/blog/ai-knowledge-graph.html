How to Get Your Brand Into the AI Knowledge Graph

AI engines trust entities they can resolve in a knowledge graph. Here's how to become a recognized, well-connected entity.

By GeoPageScan·Jun 22, 2026·4 min read

What the knowledge graph does

A knowledge graph maps entities — people, organizations, products — and their relationships. When AI can place you in that graph, it cites you with confidence and attributes you correctly.

Become a resolvable entity

Add Organization and Person schema with sameAs links to authoritative profiles (LinkedIn, Crunchbase, Wikidata), and keep your name, description and details consistent across the web.

Earn third-party validation

Mentions, reviews, directory listings and — where warranted — a Wikidata entry all strengthen your node in the graph.

Frequently asked questions

How do I get into the AI knowledge graph?⌄
Define your entity with Organization/Person schema and sameAs links, keep details consistent across the web, and earn third-party validation like reviews and directory listings.
Does Wikidata help AI visibility?⌄
Yes, when warranted. A Wikidata entry is a strong, machine-readable entity signal that AI engines trust.
What is sameAs?⌄
A schema property that links your entity to its official profiles, letting machines confirm you're the same entity across sites.
