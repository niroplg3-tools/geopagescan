AEO for E-Commerce: How to Get Your Products Recommended by AI

Product schema, review markup and structured specs that win AI shopping answers like "what's the best [product] for [use]".

By GeoPageScan·Mar 12, 2026·9 min read

AI is the new shelf

Shoppers increasingly ask an AI "what's the best X for Y". To be recommended, your products must be machine-readable, not buried in images and JavaScript.

Product + Offer schema

Mark up price, availability, brand, GTIN and key attributes with Product and Offer JSON-LD. Add AggregateRating and Review so trust signals travel with the product.

Structured specs

Put specifications in real HTML tables, not images. AI can read a table; it can't read a JPEG of one.

Frequently asked questions

What schema do product pages need for AI?⌄
Product with Offer (price, availability, brand) plus AggregateRating and Review. Add structured specs in HTML tables for machine readability.
Why aren't my products recommended by AI?⌄
Usually because key details live in images or JavaScript. Expose price, specs and reviews as crawlable HTML with Product schema.
Do reviews help product AEO?⌄
Strongly. AggregateRating and Review markup let AI factor your ratings into shopping recommendations.
