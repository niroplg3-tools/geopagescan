Winning Google AI Overviews in 2026: What Actually Works

Google's AI Overviews answer the query before the blue links. Here's how to be one of the sources they synthesize — and keep your organic visibility.

By GeoPageScan·Jun 4, 2026·4 min read

What are Google AI Overviews?

AI Overviews are the AI-generated summaries Google places above traditional results for many queries. They synthesize several sources into a direct answer — and link to the pages they drew from.

Appearing as a cited source is the new top-of-page real estate.

How to become an AI Overview source

Keep strong classic SEO (crawlability, topical authority, Core Web Vitals), allow Google-Extended, and structure content as clear question-and-answer blocks with supporting facts.

Pages that already rank well and answer the query concisely are the most likely to be pulled in.

Protect your click-through

Because AI Overviews can reduce clicks, lead with value the summary can't fully replace — depth, tools, original data — so users still have a reason to visit.

Frequently asked questions

What is Google-Extended?⌄
Google-Extended is the robots.txt control for whether your content can be used by Google's generative AI (Gemini and AI Overviews). Allow it to be eligible as a source.
How do I appear in AI Overviews?⌄
Maintain strong technical SEO and topical authority, allow Google-Extended, and structure content as concise question-and-answer blocks with specific supporting facts.
Do AI Overviews hurt traffic?⌄
They can reduce clicks for purely informational queries. Offset this with depth, original data and tools that give users a reason to click through.
