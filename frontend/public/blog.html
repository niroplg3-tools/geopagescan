FAQ

GEO & AEO, answered

What is GeoPageScan's blog about?⌄
Playbooks and guides on GEO (Generative Engine Optimization), AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and the technical SEO signals that make AI engines understand and cite your website.
What is the difference between GEO, AEO and SEO?⌄
SEO optimizes for ranking in a list of links. AEO optimizes for being the single direct answer, including voice. GEO optimizes for being understood and cited by generative AI engines like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and Perplexity.
How do I get my website cited by AI?⌄
Publish an llms.txt, add Organization and FAQPage schema, allow AI crawlers in robots.txt, and write answer-first, factual content. Run a free GeoPageScan audit to see exactly what to fix.
Is GeoPageScan free to use?⌄
Yes. Enter any URL with no login or credit card and get a full scored AI-visibility report with prioritized, copy-ready fixes.
How often should I re-audit my site?⌄
Re-scan after each fix to measure the lift, and run a fresh audit monthly — the AI search landscape and crawler list evolve quickly.
